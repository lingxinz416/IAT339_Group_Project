Art direction:

Art direction: 3D reality is the art direction we want to achieve. The layer superposition creates the depth of the website and makes it more realities. Also, the 3D reality increases the enjoyment of the website, which encourages the user to stay on the website. Base on the original Nextfix website, we found out that the combination of white and red is mainly used, which easy for catching the attention of the audience. So, we will mainly use red and white colour for our web design.
